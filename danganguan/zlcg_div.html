1922年12月10日中国政府收回青岛，胶澳商埠开始拍卖官地，对八大关和太平角一带进行绿化，一些别墅建筑陆续建成。1929年青岛设为特别市，由国民政府行政院直辖，成为北方经济和文化重镇。荣成路东一带被规划为“特别规定建筑地”，八大关区域建设由此大规模兴起。1931年青岛市工务局颁布《青岛暂行建筑规则》，对八大关区域的建筑做出详细规定，逐步形成这一区域独具特色的建筑风格。 On December 10, 1922 when Chinese government took back Qingdao, KiauTschou trading port began to sell public land. In Badaguan and Taipingjiao area, urban greening was started and a few villas were built. In 1929, Qingdao was set up as a special city under the direct administration of national government and developed into a key economic and cultural town in north China. The land in the east of Rongcheng Road was planned as “specially regulated construction land”, which triggered a construction boom. In 1951, Qingdao construction administration formulated Interim Construction Rules of Qingdao and rolled out specifications over the buildings in Badaguan. Unique architecture was hence developed in this area.

1930年青岛特别市工务局颁布的《关于规定特别规定区（荣城路、黄海路、汇泉路）建筑日期周知的提案布告》

八大关被公认为科学性与艺术性兼备的城市规划范例，“尊重自然、契合地景”是八大关规划的基本法则。 Badaguan is widely recognized as a model case that integrates science with art. Its urban planning holds the principle of respecting nature and blending into the landscape.

1931年青岛市政府制定颁布《青岛市暂行建筑规则》，其中第九章，对“特别区域”建筑高度、建筑密度、面积、建筑样式、围墙厕所以及外饰面等都做了进一步的严格规定。还特别强调建筑主体、围墙与园林的设计，避免同一条街出现同样的建筑。 In 1931, Qingdao Government promulgated the Interim Rules of Construction of Qingdao, in the ninth chapter of which, specifications were made on the construction height, density, floor area, architectural style, fences, toilets and façade in the “special area”. It was noted that the design of main buildings, fences and gardens should not be repeated on the same street.

严宏溎 (1890~1946)，字仲洁，安徽含山人。毕业于北京大学土木工程系，后公费留学美国康乃尔大学，获硕士学位。1923-1934年间历任青岛市政府工务局工程师、工务局第二科科长、工务局副局长等职，并兼任青岛大学土木工程系教授。作为当时中国一流土木工程和城建专家，严宏溎对青岛城市和八大关的规划建设做出重要贡献。 Yan Honggui (courtesy name: Zhongjie), born in Hanshan of Anhui Province, majored in Civil Engineering in Peking University and then obtained master degree in Cornell University via government-sponsored overseas program. In 1923-1934, Mr. Yan took the position as the engineer, section chief and deputy director of Qingdao construction administration, and worked as part-time professor of civil engineering in Qingdao University as well. As China’s prestigious expert in civil engineering and urban planning, Mr. Yan made great contributions to the planning and construction of Badaguan area and Qingdao’s downtown as a whole.

“特别规定建筑地”一开始就设计了10条道路，最早建成的是山海关路，后来其他九条路相继开辟。原本命名为临沂一路、临沂二路、临沂三路、涛雒路、青口路、靖海路、石岛路、成山路、里岛路，后改以关隘为名，与山海关路组成10条道路。至30年代末,10条道路修建完成。自南向北7条：山海关路、居庸关路、临淮关路、正阳关路、函谷关路、嘉峪关路、武胜关路；自西向东3条：韶关路、宁武关路、紫荆关路，形成了“南北七关”和“东西三关”。 Ten roads have been planned in the specially regulated land, with Shanhaiguan Road completed first and the other ninebuiltsuccessively. The original names of the ten roads were: Linyi 1st Road, Linyi 2nd Road, Linyi 3rd Road, Taoluo Road, Qingkou Road, Jinghai Road, Shidao Road, Chengshan Road, Lidao Road (later changed into Guan’ai) and Shanhaiguan Road. By the end of the 1930s, all the ten roads hadbeen completed, with seven north-southward ones(Shanhaiguan Road, Juyongguan Road, Linhuaiguan Road, Zhengyangguan Road, Hanguguan Road, Jiayuguan Road and Wushengguan Road) and three east-westward ones (Shaoguan Road, Ningwuguan Road, Zijingguan Road). Since “Guan” in Chinese means pass, there is a saying that Badaguan has seven vertical passes and three horizontal passes.

1933年青岛市工务局发布《特别建筑规划地的路名表》。共有道路12条，其中10条为带“关”字的道路 According to the Road Namelist of the Specially Regulated Area published by Qingdao construction administration in 1933, there were 12 roads in this area, ten of which were named by Guan (pass).

1929年之后，八大关太平角区域一直称“特别建筑规定地”。而“八大关”名称最早见于1948年9月12日青岛《民言报》上一个小广告《征求空地启事》-仿真报纸、1949年3月，青岛居民在八大关挖树被农林事务所警察队处罚的档案，表明当时“八大关”称谓已得到政府部门的认可。-仿真档案、八大关做为地理区域概念，最早出现于1958年青岛日报社出版的《青岛游览手册》“湛山疗养区一章”中。 Since 1929, Badaguan and Taipingjiao area has been designated as “specially regulated construction land”. The name of “Badaguan” was first seen on a unnoticeable land-seeking advertisement published on Qingdao Minyan Newpaper on Sep. 12, 1948 —artificial newspaper; In March 1949, the file recording a local resident punished for digging trees in Badaguan, proved that the name “Badaguan” was recognized by local authority—artificial file; Badaguan was first mentioned as a geographical concept in the“Chapter of Zhanshan resort area” of Qingdao Guide published by Qingdao Daily in 1958.

八大关被誉为“万国建筑博览会”，艺术风格多样化与文化背景多元化是其基本特征。古希腊式、哥特式、巴洛克式、国际式等各国建筑风格皆有展现。融合了中国建筑元素和营造法式，体现了东西方文化理念的交汇，这些建筑已成为青岛最宝贵的文化遗产之一。 Badaguan is reputed as “Expo of World Architecture” for its varied artistic styles and diverse culture backgrounds. Greek, Gothic, baroque and international style can all be found here. The exotic buildings also exploited Chinese architectural elements and techniques, and represented the integration of eastern and western culture, leaving the city one of the most valuable heritages. 到1933年，八大关别墅区已经成型。（1930年代八大关一带鸟瞰） By 1933, Badaguan villa area hadtaken shape.
