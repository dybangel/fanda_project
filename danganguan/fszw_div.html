邓小平下榻的山海关路9号，建于1943年，俄国建筑师尤力甫设计，初为美国总领事署别墅。许多党和国家领导人、外国首脑和国际友人等曾在此居住，被称为青岛的“钓鱼台”。 No. 9 Shanhaiguan Road where Deng Xiaoping stayed, was built in 1943. It was designed by Russian architect Yourieff and used to be a villa owned by American Consulate-General in Qingdao. A few of party and state leaders, foreign heads of states and international elites have stayed here. It is hence known as the “Diaoyutai State Gust House” in Qingdao.

1979年7月29日，邓小平在东海饭店参加了海军党委扩大会议，发表了《思想路线政治路线的实现要靠组织路线来保证》的重要讲话，这篇讲话后来被收入《邓小平文选》第二卷。 On July 29, 1979, Deng Xiaoping attended the navy’s enlarged CPC meeting, and delivered the important speech as“Organization Line, the Guarantee of Ideological Line and Political Line”. The speech was later compiled into Book II of Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping.

东海饭店,位于汇泉路7号。建于1933年，上海新瑞和洋行（英）打样间设计。初名四海饭店，一度是中国北方最大、设施最完备的假日宾馆。 Built in 1933, Donghai Hotel was located in No. 7 Huiquan Road and designed by Davies, Brooke & Gran Architects. It was formerly known as “Si Hai Hotel” and used to be the largest and best-equipped holiday hotel in north China.

1979年7月26日至31日，邓小平在青岛召开省、市委书记会议，发表了《思想路线政治路线的实现要靠组织路线来保证》等一系列重要讲话，游览了崂山，视察了北海舰队航空兵某部，勾画了引黄济青蓝图。八大关作为邓小平在青岛的居住地和主要活动场所，又一次见证了伟人风采。 DuringJuly 26 to31, 1979, Deng Xiaoping held a meeting of provincial and municipal party secretaries in Qingdao and made a series of important speeches including “Organization Line, the Guarantee of Ideological Line and Political Line”. He also toured Laoshan Mountain, inspected North China Sea Fleet and drew the blueprint of diverting the Yellow River into Qingdao. Deng Xiaoping stayed in Badaguan and most events he attended were held in this area as well. Again, Badaguan witnessed the glamour of the great leader.

The speech was later compiled into Book II of Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping.

青岛市高度重视八大关的保护工作。2001年“八大关近代建筑”被国务院公布为全国重点文物保护单位以来，青岛市对八大关进行了系统性的规划和全面保护。 Qingdao attaches great importance to the protection of Badaguan. In 2001, Badaguan complex was enlisted by State Council in the category of National Sites to be Protected for Their Historical and Cultural Value. Since then, Qingdao has carried out sustainable planning and overall protection.
