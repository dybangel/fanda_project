1898年，胶澳总督府中国人事务专员单维廉拟定的青岛首部土地法规《置买田地章程》施行，维多利亚湾（今汇泉湾）一带土地相继售租，其西北部海岸的建筑很快形成规模。此为1903年前后维多利亚湾别墅区照片。 In 1898, Kiaochow Land and Tax Statute, the first land policy regulation, was put into force. According to this Statute compiled by Wilhelm Schrameier, The Chinesecommissionerof KiauTschou government,the land around Victoria Bay (today’s Huiquan Bay)was sold and rent, and the urban complex grew soon along its northwestern shore.The picture shows the villa area around Victoria Bay in 1903

维多利亚湾别墅区形成的同时，维多利亚湾以东、太平山南麓修建了伊尔梯斯兵营和矿泉水厂，南部沿海区域尚处于原生状态，由于地处海防要地，1902年德国海军在此修建了会姓岬炮台。 While the villa area took shape around Victoria Bay, Iltis kasernen (Iltis barracks) and mineral water factory were built in the south of Taiping Hill (east of Victoria Bay). The coastal area then in the south remained untapped. In 1902, German navy built Huixingjia fortress for coastal defense.

1902年维多利亚湾开辟为海水浴场。汇泉湾以坡缓沙细、水清浪静的特色而驰名，号称“东亚第一”浴场，是当时侨民和国内外游客避暑休闲的场所。 Victoria bay was turned into a bathing beach in 1902. Huiquan Bay won its title as No. 1 Bathing Beach of East Asia for the gentle slope,fine sand, clear water and clam waves.It was a popular leisure destination for the expatriates and tourists from home and abroad.

沙滩饭店，旧译海滨旅馆，1907年建成，面朝汇泉海水浴场，是当时八大关欧洲“田园式”的旅游度假酒店，有大小三十余个房间，是当时八大关周边最大的商业建筑。1912年孙中山来青岛时下榻于此。 The Strand Hotel, built in 1907,faced Huiquan Bathing Beach and was a holiday-making hotel in Badaguan featured with European countryside style. Equipped with over 30 rooms, the hotel was the then largest commercial building in this area. Sun Yat-sen stayed in this hotel in 1912.

1912年9月28-30日，孙中山先生莅青期间，出席了三江会馆的华商集会、参观青岛特别高等学堂（德华大学）、青岛基督教青年会、参加广东会馆同乡宴会，多次发表演讲。 During Mr. Sun Yat-sen’s stay in Qingdao from September 28 to 30, 1912, he participated in a Chinese businessmen’s meeting at Sam Kiang Huay Kwan (chamber of commerce),visited Deutsch-Chinesische Hochschule and Young Men’s Christian Association in Qingdao, attended a Cantonese townfellow banquet and made several speeches.

梵都森别墅位于今太平角一路17号，建于1919年，是八大关区域现存最早的别墅。业主为美国牧师梵都森（Van Deusen）。后因多次加建、改建，现今面貌已发生较大变化。 Built in 1919, Van Deusen Villa was located in No. 17, Taipingjiao 1st Road and was the earliest existing villa in Badaguan area. The owner was American priest Van Deusen. The villa has changed a lot after several times of expansion and revision.

1912年青岛市区规划图上，深色部分是建成区。当时八大关区域出现新的海水浴场，即今第二海水浴场，面积相对较小，设施简陋。德国与美国教会在伊尔蒂斯角（今太平角）建造了一座别墅。 As shown in Qingdao’s urban plan drawings in 1912, the dark section was built-up area. A new bathing beach---No. 2 Bathing Beach was developed but remained small and simple.German and American churches built a villa in Iltis corner (today’s Taipingjiao).
