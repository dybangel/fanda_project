青岛气候温和，景色秀美，是中国著名的避暑胜地。新中国成立后，八大关一带被确定为“全国性疗养区”，规模进一步扩大，接待了众多的党和国家领导人、外国友人、科学家、文学家、艺术家、劳动模范和群众代表。 Endowed with mild climate and beautiful scenery, Qingdao is a popular health resort in China. Since the founding of New China, Badaguan has been defined as a National Health Resort and hence undergone several expansions. The area received many party and state leaders, foreign guests, scientists, authors, artists, model works and representatives from the public

1952年中共青岛市委给中共山东分局的电文中，提出将八大关、太平角、湛山大路一带开辟为疗养区。中央各部门疗养院和青岛疗养院优先购买此地区别墅，用于干部和群众休养，至1956年，八大关疗养院达到18个。In the telegraph to the CPC Shandong branch in 1952, the CPC Qingdao Committee proposed to makethe area around Badaguan, Taipingjiao and Zhanshanda Road as a health resort. The sanatoriums of central government and Qingdao had the priority to buy the villas for the rehabilitation of officials and the public. By 1956, there were 18 sanatoriums in Badaguan.

1957年7月12日至8月11日，毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德等中央领导来到青岛，召开了中央政治局常委会议和政治局扩大会议。在青期间，毛泽东完成了《一九五七年夏季的形式》一文,接见了缅甸国会访华代表团，和青岛市党政军干部合影留念，周恩来受毛泽东委托视察海军，刘少奇、朱德等中央领导人分别视察了工厂、码头和青岛市容。 From July 12 to August 11, 1957, Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai and Zhu De came to Qingdao for a meeting of Political Bureau Standing Committee and an enlarged meeting of the Political Bureau. During his stay in Qingdao, Mao Zedong finished his paper “The Situation in the Summer of 1957”, met the delegation of Myanmar MPs, took group picture with Qingdao officials of the party, government and army. Zhou Enlai, entrusted by Mao Zedong, inspected the navy. Liu Shaoqi and Zhu De inspected factories, harbours and the downtown respectivel

历代青岛的城市管理者都很重视八大关的绿化。1924年已经开始在太平角一带种植树木，有计划地进行绿化。1931年开始将未建设的地块留为绿地，与区域内的河道、松林一起，构成了良好的景观绿化环境。1959年，青岛城市建设局在市区绿化计划中提出：疗养区是重点绿化、美化、香化地区。从50年代末开始，采取专业绿化和发动群众的形式加大绿化力度，形成了八大关“看花辨时、闻香识路”的特色。 Qingdao municipalitiesalways put great emphasis on Badaguan’sgreening work. Since 1924, greening has been started in Taipingjiao area according to the set plan. Since 1931, undeveloped land lots have been planned as greenland, creating a sound natural landscape together with the rivers and pine woods. In 1959, Qingdao Bureau of Urban Construction highlighted in its city-wide greening plan that the resort area should be prioritized in the projects to make the city greener, more beautiful and with more flowers. Since the end of the 1950s, Badaguan has formed the feature of “telling the season by blossom, sensing the direction by smell” as professional means of greening were employed and citizens widely joined the campaign.
